Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our apps and services.

Information Collection

We collect information you provide directly, such as when you create an account, interact with specific apps features, or contact support. Additionally, we collect certain information automatically, such as your IP address, device information, and usage data when you interact with our apps and services.

Why We Collect Data

We collect data for the following reasons:

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To ensure the safety and security of our users
  • To run analytics and understand how users interact with our products
  • To serve advertising tailored to your preferences and interests

Use of Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide you with the services you requested
  • Enhance our services and user experience
  • Communicate with you regarding updates, offers, and support
  • Conduct internal analytics and research to improve our products
  • Serve personalized ads and offers

Legal Basis for Data Processing

Our legal basis for processing your data includes:

  • Consent: We rely on your consent for data processing activities related to personalized advertising and third-party analytics. You can withdraw your consent at any time from the “settings” page in our various applications.
  • Legitimate Interests: We process data for our legitimate interests, such as improving our services and conducting internal analytics.
  • Legal Obligations: We process data to comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements.

Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, or with your consent. We may share data with analytics and advertising partners to help improve our services and provide relevant ads.

With Whom May Your Data Be Shared & Why?

We are not selling your personal information, but we need to share it with third parties to deliver the best service possible. Our partners include:

  • Ad Partners: That allow us to monetize the ad inventory of our apps and provide users with free products and services.
    • Admob
  • Analytics Companies: That provide us with tools and technologies that allow us to better understand how users interact with our services and will help us improve it.
    • Adjust
    • Crashlytics
    • Firebase
    • GameAnalytics
    • Mixpanel
  • Authorities: Only to the extent we are obliged to by applicable laws and regulations.

Security

We implement security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access and misuse. However, no system can be completely secure, so we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

Data Retention

Personal data collected, received, and processed for the purposes described in this policy is not kept longer than necessary. We retain user-level data for no more than 13 months, except for data collected for first-party analytics, which can be stored for up to 25 months.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If we become aware that a child under 16 has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete such data.

International Transfers

Some of our partners and processors are located outside of the European Union. In such cases, we ensure that:

  • The personal data is transferred to countries recognized as offering an equivalent level of protection, or
  • Appropriate safeguards are in place, such as the European Commission's standard contractual clauses.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can request a copy of your personal data.
  • Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: You can request deletion of your personal data.
  • Portability: You can request your data in a portable format.
  • Objection: You can object to data processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: You can withdraw your consent at any time.
